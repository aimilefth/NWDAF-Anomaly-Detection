SHAP **Time Series** plots help identify how different variables influence a model's predictions over time. They highlight the most problematic time windows, meaning periods when the model shows higher error or uncertainty. These plots show how **features** affect the model's prediction at specific moments, allowing for the observation of fluctuations in their importance throughout the time series. The features are represented by their contributions, which can be positive or negative, and show whether the impact of a variable is constant or changes over time.
